I am a PhD student at the [Shanghai Center for Mathematical Sciences](https://scms.fudan.edu.cn), Fudan University. My advisor is [Professor Jun Li](https://scms.fudan.edu.cn/info/2661/2694.htm). 

Starting in October 2026, I will be a postdoc at the [Max Planck Institute for Mathematics](https://www.mpim-bonn.mpg.de), where my mentor will be [Professor Daniel Huybrechts](https://www.math.uni-bonn.de/~huybrech/).

My research interests lie in algebraic geometry and related fields, with a focus on moduli spaces of sheaves and enumerative geometry.
